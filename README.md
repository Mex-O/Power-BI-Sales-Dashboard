
# Power-BI-Sales-Dashboard
An interactive Power BI Sales Dashboard analyzing £2.29M in revenue, featuring regional performance tracking, temporal sales trends, and AI-driven influencer analysis.

# Project Description
This project demonstrates the end-to-end development of a Sales Performance Dashboard using Power BI. The analysis covers a total sales volume of £2,297,200.86 with a total quantity of 9,994 items sold. The dashboard provides a multi-layered view of business health, from high-level executive summaries to deep-dive regional analysis.

# Core Components

Sales Trends: Analyzes how sales trended over the months, showing a significant peak in September and November.



Regional Insights: Identifies California as the top-performing state by sales, with South Dakota at the bottom of the rankings.


Operational Analysis: Breaks down sales by Ship Mode (Standard Class, Second Class, First Class, and Same Day) across various product categories.



Customer Segmentation: Tracks the ranking of Consumer, Corporate, and Home Office segments over time.


Predictive Analytics: Features a Key Influencers visual to determine regional drivers; for example, being located in Florida increases the likelihood of a region being "South" by 7.77x.


# Visualizations Included

Line Charts: Monthly sales performance.


Bar Charts: Top 10 and Bottom 10 states by sales volume.



Heat Maps/Bubble Maps: Geographic distribution of sales across the United States.


Decomposition Tree: A breakdown of sales by Category and Ship Mode.


Matrix Tables: State-level breakdown of shipping preferences.
